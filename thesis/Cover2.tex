\textbf{ABSTRACT}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{justify}
This thesis presents the design and implementation of a decentralized freelance marketplace built on the Solana blockchain. The platform enables trustless job posting, bidding, escrow payments, and reputation tracking without the need for centralized intermediaries. The system implements a hybrid dual-flow hiring mechanism supporting both job-first and freelancer-first approaches. Key features include real-time notifications, advanced filtering and search capabilities, automatic role detection, and SOLID architecture principles for maintainability and scalability. The platform achieves significant improvements in freelancer discoverability (5x increase) while maintaining high performance through Solana's high-throughput blockchain infrastructure.
\end{justify}
